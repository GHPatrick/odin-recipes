Prime Rib

What is this Image Above?

It's a prime rib roast. Yes that's right, it's hard to believe. It's expensive, filled with flavor, and is good for any holiday meal.

You'd think I'm just writing nonsense here to show off how I write paragraphs, but really I'm just passionate about my prime rib roasts.

Ingredients

  • Prime Rib, 10+ lbs
  • Thyme
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Red Wine

Steps

  1. Let the roast reach room temp
  2. Preheat oven to 250
  3. Arrange and utilize ingredients on the roast
  4. Place in oven until thermometer reads 118 degrees
  5. Remove from oven, let rest 30 minutes
  6. Heat oven to 500 degrees
  7. Roast the beef for 15 minutes until brown
  8. Congratulations
