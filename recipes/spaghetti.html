Spaghetti

Spaghetti Code is regretti code

Tony Soprano ate a lot of this on screen. This ain't gabagool, this is spaghet.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesean
  • Beef Broth
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Heat a pan/pot on medium heat
  2. Add olive oil
  3. Mince onion, garlic, celery, carrots
  4. Brown beef in pan
  5. Add the minced vegetable mixture
  6. Sautee until fragrent
  7. Add sauce
  8. Add beef broth
  9. Simmer until the sauce is reduced and thick
  10. Cook spaghetti until al dente
  11. Serve
